CRISPY CHICKEN CAESAR WRAP

DINNER       MAIN COURSE

Prep Time: 30 min          Cook Time: 30 min          Total Time: 1 hour          Yields: 2 wraps

Why have just a Caesar salad when you can enjoy all those flavors wrapped up in a warm, cozy tortilla? This crispy chicken Caesar salad wrap is everything you love about the classic, but even better—think crunchy chicken tenders, creamy dressing, and a crispy parmesan-crusted tortilla. It's like taking your favorite salad and making it so much more satisfying!



Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken tenderloins (about 8 to 9 strips)
  • vegetable oil, for frying

CRISPY CHICKEN - DRY

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

CRISPY CHICKEN - WET

  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup pickle juice
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • sprinkle of salt
  • sprinkle of black pepper

CAESAR DRESSING

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons miced garlic
  • 2 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup parmesan, freshly grated

OTHER

  • 3 to 4 large flour tortillas
  • 4 to 5 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • sprinkle of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup garted parmesan, for the crust
  • oil spray

INSTRUCTIONS

DRESSING
  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, buttermilk, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, yellow mustard, anchovy paste, dried parsley, black pepper, and freshly grated parmesan cheese until smooth and well-combined.
DRY
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, mustard powder, black pepper, and baking powder. Set aside.
WET
  1. In a deep bowl, whisk together buttermilk, egg, pickle juice, salt, black pepper, and baking powder.
  2. Add 3 to 4 tablespoons of the wet batter into the dry and mix around to create a flaky texture. Set aside.
BREADING AND FRYING
  1. Dip each tender first in the dry, then into the wet, then back into the dry - using one hand for the wet only and one hand for dry only. Make sure to let any excess fall off before dipping into the next.
  2. Once all the tenders are breaded, allow to rest for 10 to 15 minutes before frying.
  3. Heat oil to about 350°F in a frying pan, about 1.5 to 2 inches high.
  4. Drop the tenders in, making sure not to overcrowd them. Fry in 2 to 3 batches. Fry until golden brown all around, about 7-8 minutes, making sure to flip over halfway.
  5. Transfer to a paper towel to drain any excess oil.
ASSEMBLY AND TOASTING
  1. In a large mixing bowl, add a generous drizzle of the dressing to your taste, lettuce, parsley, black pepper, Italian seasoning, and croutons. Toss together well.
  2. To the center of the tortilla, add a spoonful of the Caesar dressing and spread it around.
  3. Add about 1 cup of the Caesar salad mix. Place the tenders on top, about 3. Add another cup of the Caesar salad on top.
  4. Wrap the tortilla around very tightly by folding the left and right sides in and then rolling over from one end to the other to seal it up.
  5. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Spray with oil and add a generous sprinkle of parmesan cheese.
  6. Place the wrap on top of the cheese so it sticks on and let it crisp up. Once golden and crispy around the edges, repeat on the other side.
  7. Slice the wrap in half and enjoy while fresh and crispy!




LEMON LOAF CAKE

DESSERT

Prep Time: 1 hour          Cook Time: 1 hour          Total Time: 2 hours          Yields: 10 slices

If you love the lemon loaf cake from Starbucks, be prepared to be blown away by this copycat recipe! It’s packed with bright citrusy lemon flavor and the cake is incredibly moist and soft just like the real thing but even better. Drizzled with the most delicious glaze on top, this cake is surprisingly so easy to make and perfect for the summer coming up!



INGREDIENTS

LEMON CAKE

  • 1 3/4 cup all=purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3.2 ounces package lemon instant pudding
  • 1 1/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest

LEMON ICING

  • 2 cups confectioners sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. To a large mixing bowl, add the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and lemon instant pudding. Whisk together well.
  3. To another bowl, add the sugar, eggs, vegetable oil, sour cream, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Beat together until smooth.
  4. Now add all the dry ingredients to the wet. Combine together until you have a batter.
  5. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper and transfer the batter.
  6. Bake for 55 minutes to 1 hour until a toothpick comes out clean in the center.
  7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for about an hour before glazing.
  8. To a bowl, combine confectioners sugar with lemon juice and milk to create a glaze.
  9. Once the cake is cooled, add the glaze on top, adding as little or as much as you'd like.
  10. Let the frosting set for 5 minutes and then slice.
